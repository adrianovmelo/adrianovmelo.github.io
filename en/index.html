Practical case

AI as an accelerator of clarity, focus and control

In a recent Lean Agile Portfolio and Delivery & Engineering Intelligence role, AI was used to reduce operational noise and improve decision quality by expanding analysis, synthesis and monitoring capacity.

1. Better structured refinement

AI reviewed epics and user stories, suggesting acceptance criteria, risks, dependencies and key questions before delivery.

2. Clarity for execution

Stories reached teams with clearer context, objective scope, expected value and desired outcomes.

3. OKR check-ins

Through Jira queries, AI consolidated status, latest progress, blockers and attention points.

The main gain was turning scattered information into objective conversations: less time searching for data, more time discussing decisions and next steps.
